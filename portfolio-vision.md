# Digital Mills — Portfolio Vision

Digital Mills exists to empower small councils, community groups, and local organisations with simple, modern, low‑cost digital services.  
Our mission is to replace outdated paper‑based and fragmented online services with accessible, sustainable, open‑source platforms that can be deployed rapidly and maintained easily.

This portfolio vision aligns all solutions — Parish Council, Allotments, Cemeteries, and future offerings — under a single strategic direction focused on reuse, accessibility, operational simplicity, and community value.

---

## Strategic Themes (SMART-aligned)

### 1. Digital Empowerment for Local Communities  
Enable small councils and community groups to adopt modern digital services quickly and affordably.  
**Key Results:**  
- Launch 3 fully functional solution platforms by Q4.  
- Reduce onboarding time to under 2 hours by Q3.  
- Provide a no-code admin prototype with 5 features by Q3.

---

### 2. Repeatable Platforms Over One-Off Builds  
Shift from bespoke builds to reusable, scalable platforms.  
**Key Results:**  
- Deliver a shared design system by Q3.  
- Implement shared infrastructure templates by Q2.  
- Create 10 reusable components by Q3.  
- Reduce duplicated code by 50% by Q4.

---

### 3. Open, Transparent, Accessible Public Services  
Ensure all solutions meet high standards of openness, accessibility, and compliance.  
**Key Results:**  
- Achieve WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for all solutions by Q3.  
- Publish documentation openly via GitHub Pages by Q2.  
- Provide structured open data endpoints for 3 datasets by Q4.

---

### 4. Operational Simplicity & Low‑Cost Maintenance  
Minimise hosting, deployment, and long-term maintenance overhead.  
**Key Results:**  
- Zero manual deployment steps by Q3.  
- Automated CI/CD pipelines across all solutions by Q2.  
- Reduce hosting to <£15 per solution per month by Q4.  
- Add monitoring/uptime alerts by Q4.

---

### 5. Accelerated Delivery Through Reuse & Automation  
Deliver new community solutions twice as fast via automation and templating.  
**Key Results:**  
- Provide a full starter template reducing setup to <10 minutes by Q2.  
- Roll out GitHub Project templates for new solutions by Q2.  
- Deliver two new vertical solutions by Q4.  
- Auto-generate 20% of boilerplate code using AI/Copilot by Q3.

---

## Portfolio Value Streams

### 1. Community Website Platform  
A flow of value for delivering public-facing digital services (Parish, Allotments, Cemeteries).  
Covers: design → template → local configuration → deployment → support → improvements.

### 2. Managed Hosting & Infrastructure  
Reusable cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, observability, and automated operations for all solutions.

### 3. Shared Components & Reusable Modules  
Cross-solution design systems, UI components, libraries, patterns, and documentation.

---

## Portfolio Vision Summary

Digital Mills will provide a family of interoperable, open-source civic digital solutions that:

- Are fast to deploy  
- Are accessible and transparent  
- Share a unified look and feel  
- Reduce cost and operational overhead  
- Empower communities to manage digital services confidently  
- Scale efficiently across small councils and local organisations  

This portfolio vision guides all future investment, prioritisation, and solution development across Digital Mills.
