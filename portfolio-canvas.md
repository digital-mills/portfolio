#  Digital Mills — Portfolio Canvas

A SAFe-aligned single-page canvas showing the structure, strategic intent, customers, value, and operating model of the Digital Mills portfolio.

---

##  1. Portfolio Purpose
Provide modular, reusable, low‑cost digital services for small councils, community groups, and local organisations.  
Reduce administrative burden, modernise civic engagement, and bring essential public-facing services online.

---

##  2. Customers & Beneficiaries

### Primary Customers
- Parish and Town Councils  
- Allotment Associations  
- Cemetery Managers and Burial Authorities  

### Users / Beneficiaries
- Residents  
- Clerks and administrators  
- Volunteers and committee members  
- Visitors, plot holders, family members

---

##  3. Value Propositions
- Rapid setup for local digital services  
- Open-source, transparent, and accessible  
- Long-term low operational cost  
- Modern, consistent design across services  
- Self-service administration  
- Automated hosting and deployment patterns  

---

##  4. Strategic Themes
See `portfolio-vision.md` for full OKR-aligned definitions.

1. Digital Empowerment for Local Communities  
2. Repeatable Platforms Over One-Off Builds  
3. Open, Transparent, Accessible Public Services  
4. Operational Simplicity & Low-Cost Maintenance  
5. Accelerated Delivery Through Reuse & Automation  

---

##  5. Value Streams

### Community Website Platform
End-to-end delivery pipeline for public-facing civic websites.

### Managed Hosting & Infrastructure
Shared IaC, CI/CD, monitoring, cost optimisation.

### Shared Components & Modules
Design system, component libraries, code templates, patterns.

---

##  6. Solutions in the Portfolio

### Current Solutions
- Parish Council Website Platform  
- Allotment Website Platform  
- Cemetery Website Platform  

### Future Solutions
- Community Halls booking system  
- Local events platform  
- Volunteer management  

---

##  7. Architectural Principles
- Reuse first  
- Documented, open standards  
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)  
- API-first expansion route  
- Automation over manual processes  
- Clear separation of Solution Intent from implementation  
- Multi-repo modularity  

---

- **EP01:** Shared Design System v1  
- **EP02:** Shared Infrastructure Templates  
- **EP03:** Community Website Starter Template  
- **EP04:** Open Data API for Civic Services  
- **EP05:** One-click Deployment Pipeline  

---

##  9. Measures of Success (Portfolio KPIs)
- Time to deploy a new solution  
- Cost per solution per month  
- Accessibility compliance scores  
- Reuse ratio of components across solutions  
- Number of councils/organisations onboarded  
- Customer satisfaction scores (admin usability)  

---

##  10. Governance Model
- Portfolio Project governs priorities, epics, themes  
- Solution Projects govern features/stories  
- Solution Repositories hold Vision & Intent  
- Shared repositories manage reusable assets  
- GitHub Projects Roadmaps track timelines  
- GitHub Pages hosts portfolio documentation  

---

## 🏁 Summary
The Digital Mills Portfolio Canvas provides a unified view of how we deliver coherent, scalable, affordable digital services to community organisations. It aligns strategy, architecture, value delivery, and operational excellence into a single direction for all current and future solutions.
